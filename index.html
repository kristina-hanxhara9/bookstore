Sarah Co-founder

"My name is Sarah, and I'm a young Eelam Tamil mother, born and raised in Italy, now living in Germany. In addition to being a midwife and a mother of two, I've always been an avid reader. We want to give our children what we missed out on during our own childhoods—beautiful, fun books that will help them discover and connect with their mother tongue. 'Mudivili' is our little dream, slowly coming to life."

Laxy Co-founder

"Hi All!! I'm Laxy, a second-generation Eelam Tamil born in Italy currently living in the UK. Growing up as a child of the diaspora, the questions 'Who am I?' and 'Where do I belong?' have always been inside me. Tamil has played a vital role in answering these questions. For me, Tamil is more than just a language—it is who I am. I'm so happy to share this journey with Sarah and to bring beautiful Tamil books into the homes of a lot of kutties."

Our Story

We are Mudivili

OUR VISION
The term Mudivili in Tamil means infinity, and so is the depth, richness and beauty of the Tamil language. At Mudivili, we are dedicated to nurture this endless journey, fostering a lifelong love for Tamil and our culture.

OUR MISSION
Nurture the love for the Tamil language through storytelling. We strive to create a vibrant community space where the Tamil language and culture are celebrated and preserved for the future generations.

OUR PURPOSE
Ensure the rich heritage of Tamil is passed down to future generations, giving children the opportunity to explore, enjoy and own their language. By doing so, we want to preserve the stories, culture, and history embedded within the Tamil language.
